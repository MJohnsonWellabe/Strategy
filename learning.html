How To Use This Section

What Belongs Here

📋 Strategic Decisions

Major decisions that commit resources, change direction, or resolve a material ambiguity. Not every management meeting agenda item — only decisions that future readers would need to understand why we are on the path we are on.

Example: "Decided to pursue C-2 financing reinsurance with Hannover as the primary capital solution over quota share. Rationale: maintains full block returns."

⚠️ Changed Assumptions

When a key assumption that underpins the strategy changes — the strategy should adapt. Documenting the assumption shift is how leadership tracks whether the plan is still valid.

Example: "MS rerate flexibility is more constrained than modeled — state regulators pushing back in three states. Updating capital scenarios to reflect 10% rerate cap sensitivity."

🔴 Strategic Risks

Named, owned risks with a specific mechanism, trigger, and exposure. Not generic risk language. The owner is accountable for monitoring and response.

Example: "Aspida Re reduces MS flow capacity. Owner: Sara Lehan. Trigger: capacity reduction >20%. Exposure: capital plan requires restructuring, potential sales slowdown."
